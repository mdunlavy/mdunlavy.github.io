Research

Reliable high-performance computing for scientific workloads.

My research studies how GPU architectures, compiler layers, and scientific applications interact under faults, with an emphasis on reproducible evaluation.

Current Focus

I work on methods for evaluating resilience across heterogeneous GPU platforms. This includes fault injection, workflow characterization, and analysis techniques that help explain when failures become visible to applications.
