Research Groups

SPEAR Lab

The SPEAR lab studies scalable, performance-aware, and resilient computing systems for high-performance scientific applications. It is part of EVL (Electronic Visualization Lab).

Visit SPEAR

Electronic Visualization Laboratory

EVL is an interdisciplinary research laboratory at UIC focused on visualization, advanced computing, collaboration technologies, and data-intensive research.

Visit EVL

AIDQ Project

AIDQ is an Argonne National Laboratory project focused on anomaly-informed data quality for high-energy physics. My work supports ML-compatible validation pipelines, benchmarking protocols, and anomaly detection methods for multivariate detector data.

Visit Argonne

Funding

My graduate work is supported by C² the P², the Chicagoland Computational Traineeship in High Energy Particle Physics. The program trains physics and computer science students in computational tools for particle physics through coursework, seminars, lab collaborations, retreats, and research integration with partners including UIC, NIU, Argonne, and Fermilab.
